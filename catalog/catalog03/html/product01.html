Life Guard-100
RESCUE WAY : CURRENT WAY
Rescue way so far
In the event of a drowning accident, the rescue is carried out by human power by throwing the life tube provided in the lifesaving box, and a lifeboat approaches the drowning person in the distance.
For remote drowning persons, it is difficult to throw more than 7~8m of existing lifesaving boxes due to problems such as weight and volume. The golden time to save the drowning person is 3 minutes, and there are several problems with the current rescue method.
