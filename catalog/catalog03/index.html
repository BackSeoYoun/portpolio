Life Guard-100
Rescue quickly within 3 minutes of golden time!
Lifesaving rocket launcher flying up to 60m
Life Guard-100 is placed on beaches, breakwaters, valleys, and reservoirs. When a self-inflating buoyancy device is fired in the event of drowning, the projectile expands into a tube as soon as it touches the water. It is a continuous firing system that can be used in both short and long distances up to 60m and provides buoyancy to the drowning person because it can be used by anyone, regardless of age or gender.
